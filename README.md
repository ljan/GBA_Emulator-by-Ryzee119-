Screen | Raspberry Pi 3 or zero 
------------ | -------------
SDI(MOSI) | Pin 19 (GPIO10) 
SDO(MISO) | PIN 21 (GPIO9) 
SCK | Pin 23 (GPIO11) 
CS | Pin 24 (GPIO8) 
DC | Pin 22 (GPIO25) 
RESET | Pin 26 (GPIO7) 
LED | Pin 12 (GPIO18) 
VCC | Pin 1 (3.3V) 
GND | Pin 20 (GND)
